## Zenodo Submission: AI Washing Metadata Detection Method

### Title
AI Washing Detection in Public Procurement: A Metadata Framework and Evidence Patterns

### Authors
- Anton Sokolov (Tyche Institute, Tallinn, Estonia)

### Abstract
This dataset and methodology paper presents a structured approach to detecting AI washing in public procurement documents through metadata analysis. We define AI washing as the inclusion of AI-related terminology without corresponding technical implementation details. Our framework identifies patterns across 12 Baltic and Finno-Ugric jurisdiction sources, mapping to OWASP AI Top 10 and EU AI Act compliance indicators.

### Keywords
AI washing, procurement metadata, Baltic AI governance, LIMEN Atlas, evidence patterns
