# Incident Classification Taxonomy for Deepfake/Synthetic Identity Incidents

## Objective
To systematically categorize and analyze incidents involving deepfakes and synthetic identities in public-sector contexts, enabling cross-jurisdictional comparisons and evidence-based policy insights.

## Classification Dimensions
1. **Sector**
   - Government ID Systems
   - Public Procurement
   - Election Integrity
   - Healthcare
   - Law Enforcement

2. **Technique**
   - Facial Manipulation
   - Audio Cloning
   - Document Forgery (e.g., AI-generated PDFs)
   - Identity Simplication (partial synthetic attributes)

3. **Impact Vector**
   - Financial Loss
   - Reputation Damage
   - Operational Disruption
   - Legal Liability

4. **Mitigation Strategies**
   - Digital Watermarking
   - Multi-Factor Authentication
   - Blockchain Verification
   - Human-in-the-Loop Controls

## Methodology
- **Source Triangulation**: Cross-reference official reports, media coverage, and regulatory filings.
- **Language Tagging**: Mark non-English sources with translation confidence scores.
- **Temporal Analysis**: Track incident frequency and mitigation evolution over time.
