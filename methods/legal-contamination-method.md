## Legal Contamination Methodology

This method note documents the approach used to construct the contamination-risk-matrix.tsv artifact for LIMEN Boost Shard 008. The methodology focuses on identifying procedural and legal contamination risks in AI governance sources through the following steps:

1. **Jurisdiction Pair Selection**: Criteria included regulatory divergence, data sovereignty conflicts, and differences in AI governance frameworks.
2. **Conflict Score Assignment**: Scores (0.0-1.0) assigned based on:
   - Regulatory alignment/divergence
   - Data transfer restrictions
   - Enforcement mechanisms
   - Fundamental governance model differences
3. **Basis Documentation**: Each jurisdiction pair includes references to
   - Relevant legal texts (EUR-Lex, CJEU rulings, ECHR decisions)
   - National AI strategies and implementation gaps
   - International treaty obligations and conflicts

The contamination risk matrix serves as the foundation for visualizing legal overlaps and conflicts in AI governance frameworks across jurisdictions.
