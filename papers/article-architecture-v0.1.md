# Article Architecture for LIMEN Research v0.1

## Objective
Define a structured framework for LIMEN-related research articles, ensuring alignment with academic standards and publication requirements.

## Key Sections
1. **Introduction**
   - Research context and significance
   - Objective and scope

2. **Methods**
   - Data collection and processing pipelines
   - Dashboard implementation details

3. **Related Work**
   - Overview of existing research in the field
   - Comparative analysis with LIMEN's approach

4. **Results**
   - Visualizations from the dashboard
   - Key findings and evidence tiers

5. **Discussion**
   - Interpretation of results
   - Limitations and future work

6. **Conclusion**
   - Summary of contributions
   - Implications for policy/standards

## Publication Routes
- Target journals: Nature Machine Intelligence, Journal of Artificial Intelligence Research
- Conference options: NeurIPS, ICML, ACM SIGAI
- Preprint platforms: arXiv, SSRN

## Compliance
- Adhere to Tyche Institute's publication ethics and data privacy standards.
- Ensure all claims are evidence-supported and legally vetted.
