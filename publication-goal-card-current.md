# LIMEN Publication Goal Card

Status: active from 2026-06-07

## Win Condition

Build LIMEN into a publishable and dashboard-ready observatory for AI edge
cases: long-tail incidents, misuse, security risks, agentic failures,
institutional absurdities, and normative anomalies that existing databases do
not fully capture or classify.

## First Article Package

Working title:

*LIMEN: A Public-Source Atlas of AI Edge Cases, Misuse, and Normative
Anomalies*

Likely paper types:

- data descriptor;
- methods paper;
- AI governance / responsible AI empirical article;
- security-governance bridge paper;
- attestation/provenance methods note.

## Required First-Cycle Artifacts

1. Source-family saturation ledger.
2. Seed case database with evidence tiers.
3. Crosswalk to AIID, OECD AIM, AVID, MIT AI Risk Repository, CSET AI Harm,
   MITRE ATLAS, NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act, ISO/IEC 42001, and OWASP categories.
4. Duplicate-cluster and residual-category note.
5. Legal/normative uncertainty queue.
6. Attestation evidence-envelope profile.
7. Dashboard specification and first figure/table plan.

## Claim Boundaries

LIMEN can claim public-source observations, evidence-tier assignments,
crosswalk mappings, source-family saturation estimates, and attested processing
steps.

LIMEN cannot claim total completeness, legal guilt, non-compliance, official
incident status, safety certification, or truth of source allegations unless
the narrow claim is directly supported by an authoritative public source.

## Dashboard Shape

Minimum dashboard views:

- evidence-tier funnel;
- source-family saturation map;
- jurisdiction/language map;
- taxonomy heatmap;
- security/agentic crosswalk;
- legal uncertainty matrix;
- duplicate-cluster graph;
- residual-category queue.
