# Dashboard Hook: Public-Sector Procurement Cases Visualization

## Description
Visualizes public-sector procurement cases related to AI, focusing on institutional practices and transparency issues across jurisdictions. Uncertainty levels indicate the reliability of the source material.

## Visualization Type
Interactive Choropleth Map with Filter Options

## Fields Used
- `case_id`: Unique identifier for each case.
- `jurisdiction`: Country or region where the case occurred.
- `description`: Brief summary of the procurement issue.
- `source`: Link to the source material.
- `uncertainty`: Level of uncertainty associated with the case (low, medium, high).

## Parameters
- **Filter by Jurisdiction**: Select specific countries or regions to view.
- **Filter by Uncertainty**: Show cases with low, medium, or high uncertainty levels.

## Paper/Thesis Use
Supports research questions on geographical distribution of procurement transparency and institutional accountability in AI adoption. Suitable for methods/results sections or thesis appendices.
