# Taxonomy Expansion Proposal for LIMEN Boost Shard 046

## Proposed New Categories
1. **Multilingual Contextual Failures**
   - Definition: Edge cases where AI systems produce incorrect or harmful outputs due to language-specific nuances in underrepresented languages.
   - Examples: Misinterpretation of idioms, dialect-specific errors, script-specific rendering issues.
   - Dashboard Hook: Language Coverage Matrix (expand with script and dialect dimensions).

2. **Jurisdictional Ambiguity in AI Governance**
   - Definition: Situations where AI systems operate in regions with overlapping or unclear regulatory jurisdictions.
   - Examples: Cross-border data flows, conflicting national AI strategies, unregulated tribal territories.
   - Dashboard Hook: Regulatory Sandbox Topology Map.

## Crosswalk with OECD AI Risk Taxonomy v1.2
| New Category                  | OECD Category               | Gap Identified                          |
|-------------------------------|-----------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| Multilingual Contextual Failures | AI System Failure          | Missing linguistic/script-specific risk granularity |
| Jurisdictional Ambiguity      | Governance Framework        | No coverage of cross-border/tribal scenarios |
