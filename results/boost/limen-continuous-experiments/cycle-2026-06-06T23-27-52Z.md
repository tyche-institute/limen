## Verified Article 50 text relevant to shard 007

Authoritative EUR-Lex text confirms that Article 50 contains all three routing elements needed for the shard-007 transparency/provenance frame:

1. Article 50(2): providers of AI systems generating synthetic audio, image, video, or text content must ensure outputs are "marked in a machine-readable format and detectable as artificially generated or manipulated".
2. Article 50(4): deployers of an AI system that generates or manipulates image, audio, or video content constituting a deep fake must disclose that the content has been artificially generated or manipulated.
3. Article 50(4): deployers of AI-generated or AI-manipulated text published to inform the public on matters of public interest must disclose that the text has been artificially generated or manipulated, subject to the article's stated exceptions.
