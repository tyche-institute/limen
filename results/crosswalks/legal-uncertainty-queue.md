# Legal Uncertainty Queue

- `public_sector_misuse_or_gap`: Requires confirmation of public authority involvement and compliance assessment; needs legal review.
- `normative_or_moral_outlier`: Subjective moral assessment; requires human review.
- `residual_unclassified`: Placeholder for cases not fitting existing categories; needs classification effort.
- `security_risk`: May involve systemic risk; coordination with security team required.
- `agentic_control_failure`: Involves uncontrolled agent behavior; requires policy and governance review.
