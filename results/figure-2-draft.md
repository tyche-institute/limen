# Figure 2 Draft: AI Governance Gaps Evidence Matrix

## Overview
This figure maps key AI governance gaps to evidence tiers and representative cases derived from LIMEN source observations.

## Evidence Tiers

- **Tier 2**: Indirect but validated patterns (e.g., synthetic identity fraud cases).
- **Tier 3**: Emerging signals requiring further verification.
