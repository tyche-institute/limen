## Saturation Methodology

1. **Definition**: Source-family saturation achieved when:
   - All major subcategories mapped
   - Coverage gaps documented
   - Crosswalk to LIMEN taxonomy validated

2. **Status Definitions**:
   - **Not started**: No mapping initiated
   - **Initial**: At least one mapped case
   - **Partial**: ≥50% subcategories mapped
   - **Complete**: All subcategories mapped and verified
