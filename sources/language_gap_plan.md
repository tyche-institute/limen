# Language Gap Plan

## Priority Languages
- Sami
- Welsh
- Catalan

## Potential Sources
- Sami: Nordic government portals, Sami Parliament websites, cultural institutions
- Welsh: Welsh Government publications, Welsh Language Commissioner resources
- Catalan: Generalitat de Catalunya publications, IRIAF (Institut d'Investigació i Recerca en l'Art i el Conte de la Catalangunya), Catalan cultural archives

## Search Strategy
1. Search government portals for language-specific content
2. Check academic repositories for publications in these languages
3. Identify cultural heritage archives
4. Verify access permissions and citation requirements
