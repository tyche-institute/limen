# LIMEN AI Edge-Case Atlas Taxonomy

## Legal-Procedural Contamination Patterns (Theme 008)

### Dimension: Source Authority Erosion
- **Code**: AUTH-EROSION
- **Definition**: Instances where AI system claims reference non-authoritative or deprecated legal/regulatory texts
- **Indicators**:
  - Citation of repealed laws
  - Reference to non-binding guidelines as enforceable standards
  - Mismatch between claimed jurisdiction and actual legal authority

### Dimension: Procedural Invalidation

**Code**: PROC-INVALID

**Definition**: Processes that fail to meet minimum legal procedural requirements for AI deployment

**Indicators**:
  - Lack of required impact assessments
  - Missing stakeholder consultation records
  - Non-compliant documentation formats

### Dimension: Cross-Jurisdictional Conflict

**Code**: JURIS-CONF

**Definition**: When AI systems operate across jurisdictions with conflicting legal requirements

**Indicators**:
  - Contradictory data protection requirements
  - Incompatible algorithmic accountability frameworks
  - Enforcement mechanism conflicts
